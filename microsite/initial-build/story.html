The Galaxy Awaits

Long ago, when the first explorers set foot beyond the stars, a universe of adventure began to take shape. Across the cosmic frontier, daring pioneers, cunning outlaws, and mysterious forces all carved their place among the stars. These are their stories.

Classic Astronauts

The First Explorers

They were the original dreamers, blue-suited astronauts charting the unknown with nothing but curiosity and a few trusty bricks. Their mission: explore, build, and bring peace to the galaxy. From moonbases to sleek starships, Classic Space laid the foundation for every adventure to come.

Futuron

Keepers of the Future

As humanity’s presence in space grew, a new generation of explorers emerged. Futuron united technology and harmony, constructing sleek monorails and shining domes across the stars. They believed in progress, and that the best way to move forward was together.

Blacktron

Masters of the Shadows

But not everyone played by the rules. In the deep void Blacktron thrived. Pilots in stealthy black-and-yellow ships who took what they wanted and vanished without a trace. Some say they were outlaws. Others, pioneers of freedom. Either way, their legend still lingers in the dark.

M:Tron

The Magnetic Miners

Deep beneath the surface of distant planets, M:Tron worked tirelessly to extract the galaxy’s rarest minerals. Their red-and-black ships shimmered with energy fields and magnetized cargo pods. While others fought or explored, M:Tron fueled the future, one magnetic haul at a time.

Blacktron Future Generation

Return of the Rogues

When the galaxy forgot the old Blacktron name, they came back stronger, sleeker suits, faster ships, and brighter neon. These high-tech renegades carried on the spirit of rebellion, proving that you can’t keep a good villain grounded for long.

Ice Planet

Scientists of the Frozen Frontier

Far from the sun’s warmth, a team of brilliant scientists braved icy worlds to uncover the galaxy’s secrets. Equipped with chainsaws, rocket skis, and sheer courage, the Ice Planet crew turned frozen isolation into opportunity — and made the cold look cooler than ever.
